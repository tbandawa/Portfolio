open Radio

The idea behind open Radio was to have one application to handle
my favourite stations instead of having numerous audio streaming
applications installed on my phone. I gave myself a task to design
and develop open Radio whilst discovering cool features ExoPlayer
    - station management
    - online streaming capabilities
    - media tag info
    - informative notification

Through research and studying other audio streaming
applications, i used my findings as a framework to establish
what became the visuals of open Radio.

The app’s chosen core architectures were MVVM and Hexagonal.
This approach provides a decoupled pattern that allowed me to
focus on different elements of the app (modules) while other aspects where
being finalised i.e. the lastfm API
